About Me

My name is Ericka Garcia.
I am an upcoming Graphic Designer from El Salvador, but living in Miami.

I am constantly learning to adapt to the dynamic commercial world of design.
The solutions I design are integral and conceptualized to the root.
I hate all clichés but one, "Making a difference in the world". My goal is to achieve this by doing things my way,
as I’ve always had conviction in my own beliefs and judgment,
freedom that art and design have granted me.
